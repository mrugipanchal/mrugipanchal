Abstract

People in developing countries often suffer from limitations in their career and social life due to lack of proper expression in their spoken English. This project aims to develop a speech prosody evaluator for reading fluency of school students in India. The Desktop application will present passages suitable to the studentâ€™s grade level and upon reading, the recorded speech prosody features of duration, pitch and intensity will be used to calculate a score. The scores when analyzed, will determine the reading fluency of the student. The application, which is first of its kind for English as a second language (ESL) school students, is expected to help them with pitch, loudness, tempo and rhythm skills of English speaking, which is vital for their social and career development.
